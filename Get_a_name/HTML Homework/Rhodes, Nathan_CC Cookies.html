How to make Mama’s recipe: the best chocolate chip cookies

  1. Preheat oven to 350ºF
  2. Beat butter and sugar together until light and fluffy
  3. Stir in puding mix, milk and vanilla extract
  4. Beat in eggs
  5. Add dry ingredients and stir until combined
  6. Stir in chocolate chips and nuts
  7. Place 1 1/2 inch balls of dough 2 inches apart on an un-greased cookie sheet
  8. Bake 8-10 minutes or until golden brown

EXPERT TIPS

Use an ice-cream scoop to make perfectly shaped cookies. Level off the dough on the edge of the bowl for perfectly round cookies. If you didn't think these cookies could get any better, think again! Get creative! Instead of the 2 cups semisweet chcolate chips, make with 1/2 cup each of semisweet chocolate chips, peanut butter chips or butterscotch chips.
